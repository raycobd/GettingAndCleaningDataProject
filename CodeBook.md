##Variables

The name of the variables and combination of them are described as follows:


Variable name    | Description
-----------------|------------
subjectID        | ID the subject. Its range is from 1 to 30.
activityID       | Activity ID. Its range is from 1 to 6.
activityLabel    | Activity name
Time             | Body linear acceleration and angular velocity were derived in time
Fourier          | Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) was applied
Body             | Body acceleration
Gravity          | Gravity acceleration
Accelerometer    | Source of the data Accelerometer
Gyroscope        | Source of the data Gyroscope
Jerk             | Jerk signal directed in time
Magnitude        | Magnitude calculated using Euclidean norm
Mean             | Mean values
StdDev           | Standard Deviation
Axis             | 3-axial componentes X, Y and Z
